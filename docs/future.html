Future Scope and Applications

Regarding future scope of our application, the technology and software provides far reaching implications past a tailored news and trending post website. This techonology could be used to track sentiment for movies and tv shows, guage popularity of policy changes and news stories, and search criteria and results can even be tailored to track natural disasters and aid first-responders.
